关于米仓 About Rice Granary

杭州米仓艺术中心坐落于西湖区，前身为一座建于上世纪50年代的粮仓。我们于2025年初改建并开放，旨在创造一个连接东西方当代艺术的创新平台。

The Hangzhou Rice Granary Arts Center is located in West Lake District, transformed from a 1950s grain warehouse. Renovated and opened in early 2025, we aim to create an innovative platform connecting Eastern and Western contemporary art.

愿景与使命 Vision & Mission

米仓艺术中心致力于推动艺术创新、文化交流和社区参与。我们希望成为杭州乃至中国艺术生态中的重要枢纽，通过展览、讲座、工作坊等多种形式，为艺术家和观众创造对话的可能性。

Rice Granary Arts Center is committed to promoting artistic innovation, cultural exchange, and community engagement. We aspire to be a vital hub in Hangzhou's and China's art ecosystem, creating possibilities for dialogue between artists and audiences through exhibitions, lectures, workshops, and more.
