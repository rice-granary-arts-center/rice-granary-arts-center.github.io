关于米仓 About Rice Granary

米仓艺术中心-文化记忆的同行者 The Rice Granary Arts Center: A Companion to Cultural Memory

米仓艺术中心以亚洲稻米文化为核心，致力于成为跨国艺术交流的平台。米仓位于杭州三墩镇五里塘历史街区，原为清同治三年 (1864年）的朱泰和粮油仓库，后改建为三墩粮管所碾米厂。

Rice Granary Arts Center is dedicated to Asian rice culture, aiming to serve as a transnational platform for artistic exchange. Located in the historic Wulitang district of Sandun Town, Hangzhou, it was originally the Zhu Taihe Granary, built in 1864.
